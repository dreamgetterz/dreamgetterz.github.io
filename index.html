About Debt Shop in a Box

Meet Our Founder: Troy J. Davis

Mr. Davis, the powerhouse founder of Debt Shop in a Box, is a globally recognized sales titan and industry innovator who has reshaped the fields of debt and tax relief along with student loan assistance programs. Since his early days as a top sales producer at age 15, Troy has built a legacy of excellence, generating over a billion dollars in sales, transforming 40+ companies, and mentoring thousands of reps and managers to achieve unparalleled success. A serial entrepreneur, he has successfully founded and scaled multiple debt and tax relief enterprises along with student loan assistance programs while establishing high-performing sales floors across the globe, including in Columbia, Mexico, the Philippines, India, and Belize, cementing his reputation as a visionary leader in financial services. From Top Sales Rep to Director and multiple-time owner, Troy’s elite strategies—sharpened under the guidance of world-class mentors like Tony Robbins, Andy Frisella, Ed Mylett, Alex and Leila Hormozi, Brian Tracy, Jim Rohn, and Bedros Keuilian—power the transformative Master Jedi Coaching Program, delivering exponential growth for businesses worldwide.

Beyond his professional triumphs, Troy leads a life of purpose and passion: married to his best friend Ericka in 2022, father to son Jordan (born 2023), and a devoted owner of dogs Ace and Daisy. Coming from a proud family of veterans, Troy is deeply patriotic and a staunch Second Amendment enthusiast, reflecting his commitment to American values. A fitness enthusiast, he channels his energy into basketball, martial arts, motorcycles, and mountain biking, while indulging his love for cars, shoes, watches, crypto investing, and real estate as a dedicated enthusiast. An avid traveler, Troy explores the world, drawing inspiration from its diverse cultures and landscapes.

Making a Difference

Troy is dedicated to creating a lasting impact beyond business success. That’s why Debt Shop in a Box proudly donates 10% of all proceeds to support homeless veterans and animal rescues. By partnering with us, you’re not just accessing top-tier services—you’re also helping to uplift American veterans in need and provide care for animals, contributing to meaningful change in communities across the nation.
